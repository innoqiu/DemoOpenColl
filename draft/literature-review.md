# Using AI to Proxy Communication

AI systems increasingly sit between people rather than merely helping one person
compose a private text. In these settings, the system may summarize a teammate's
position, rewrite a message for another audience, filter which updates deserve
attention, or suggest when a direct conversation is needed.

This literature review treats those cases as **AI proxy communication**: moments
where AI carries, reshapes, times, filters, or partially authors communication
across a human boundary.
