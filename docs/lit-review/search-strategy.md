# Search Strategy

Seed query:

```text
("AI-mediated communication" OR "AI proxy" OR "communication agent")
AND (collaboration OR workplace OR group work OR interpersonal)
```

Inclusion criteria:

- Mentions AI mediation between two or more people.
- Provides empirical cases, system design, or conceptual framing.
- Helps explain one mechanism: summarizing, translating, filtering, delegating,
  or negotiating communication.

Exclusion criteria:

- Purely individual writing tools with no interpersonal communication surface.
- General chatbot adoption papers without a communication boundary.

Current source target: 10 to 14 sources, with at least 6 coded in the matrix.
