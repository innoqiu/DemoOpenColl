# Communication Breakdown Taxonomy

Working categories:

1. **Compression loss**: summaries remove tone, uncertainty, or minority views.
2. **Voice drift**: AI-generated wording no longer sounds accountable to the
   sender.
3. **Timing mismatch**: automatic mediation changes the pace of response or
   creates stale context.
4. **Audience mismatch**: the proxy adapts a message for one imagined receiver
   while another receiver interprets it differently.
5. **Responsibility blur**: recipients cannot tell whether a claim came from a
   person, an AI system, or a combination.

Open question: whether "translation across expertise" belongs under audience
mismatch or deserves its own category.
