# Project Brief

Write a literature review subsection on **Using AI to Proxy Communication**.

The subsection should explain when AI systems stand in for, filter, summarize,
translate, or otherwise mediate communication between people. The review should
connect empirical examples to a small conceptual taxonomy and stay clear about
risks: loss of nuance, accountability gaps, over-personalization, and hidden
labor.

Final audience: readers of a research paper on AI-mediated collaboration.

Definition guardrail: "proxy communication" means AI acting as an intermediary
that changes what is said, when it is said, who receives it, or how accountable
the sender appears.
